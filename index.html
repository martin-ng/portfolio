About Me



Hello! A little story about me..

Until then, I've spent several years as a building construction professional improving New York City's transportation and commercial infrastructure.



Why did I switch?



I've realized the impact technology had in everyday lives and workflow productivity. I began my programming journey by self-teaching Java and Data Structures and found myself loving the new challenges. Knowing the limitless potential of technology, I took the leap of faith to leave my career and joined Fullstack Academy to kick-start my Software Engineering career.

Here are some projects I've built recently

AR World


Ever find yourself in a new city or place with little clue on what to do? AR World is an augmented reality mobile application designed to give users a more interactive and immersive way of looking for point of interests and leave reviews. AR World maps point of interests in augmented reality space with data pulled from Foursquare's API and rendered with Viro React. AR World was created in 2.5 weeks in an agile team of 4 as a capstone project.

Tech Stack: JavaScript, React Native, Viro React, Redux, Node, PostgreSQL, Foursquare API

Stockify


Stockify is a mock stocks trading platform for aspiring traders to experience trading stocks without any financial risks. Users can purchase and sell stocks, manage a portfolio's performance, and audit a transaction history with updated prices pulled from IEX Cloud API. Unit testing was integrated with Mocha and Chai to turn requirements into test cases and write code to ensure those test cases pass.

Tech Stack: JavaScript, React, Node, Redux, PostgreSQL, IEX Cloud API
